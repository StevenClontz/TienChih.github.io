If \(a_n>0\text{,}\) then there are 2 possibilities:

  • If \(n\) is even, (like \(x^2, x^4, x^6)\text{,}\) then \(a_nx^n\) cannot be negative, so \(p(x)\) will tend to positive infinity as \(x\) goes to either positive or negative infinity.

  • If \(n\) is odd, (like \(x, x^3, x^5)\text{,}\) then \(a_nx^n\) is positive as long as \(x\) is positive, so \(p(x)\) will tend to positive infinity as \(x\) goes to either positive infinity, and \(a_nx^n\) will go to negative infinity as x goes to negative infinity.
