These can be identified algebraically:

  1. When \(\textcolor{ForestGreen}{y=0}\text{,}\) we have:

    \begin{align*} y\amp =\amp 2x-3\\ \textcolor{ForestGreen}{0}\amp =\amp 2x-3\\ 3\amp =\amp 2x\\ x\amp =\amp 1.5\text{.} \end{align*}

  2. If \(\textcolor{orange}{x=3}\text{:}\)

    \begin{align*} y\amp = 2x-3\\ y\amp = 2(\textcolor{orange}{3})-3\\ y\amp = 6-3=3\text{.} \end{align*}
