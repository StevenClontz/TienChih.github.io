Definition 10.1.1 is the traditional “average”. The higher values are counterbalanced by the lower values, and it yeilds the value “in between”. In our above example, our sample mean would be

\begin{equation*} \bar{x}=\frac{1+2+2+2+2+3+3+3+3+3+3+4+4+4+5+5+6+6}{18}\approx 3.4444. \end{equation*}
