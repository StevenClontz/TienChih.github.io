Another example, lets consider the derivative of \(y=(x^2+100)^{300}\text{.}\) We COULD expand this polynomial out, but lets agree no one wants that. On the other hand by the chain rule:

\begin{align*} \frac{dy}{dx}\amp =\amp \frac{d}{dx}[(x^2+100)^{300}],\ \text{ so by the chain rule: }\\ \amp =\amp 300(x^2+100)^{299}\frac{d}{dx}[x^2+100]\\ \amp =\amp 300(x^2+100)^{299}(2x)\\ \amp =\amp 600x(x^2+100)^{299}\text{.} \end{align*}
