Item 8.3.17.6.
We note that if you eat chicken, the chance you would get sick is \(P(S|C)=\frac{5}{25}=0.2\text{.}\) If you eat fish, the chance you would get sick is \(P(S|F)=\frac{4}{10}=0.4\text{.}\) If you eat steak, the chance you would get sick is \(P(S|T)=\frac{3}{15}=0.2\text{.}\) So Fish is the most likely to get you sick.
