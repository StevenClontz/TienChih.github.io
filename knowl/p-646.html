You can see it graphically verified here: \url{https://www.desmos.com/calculator/u89fjxup7q}. For general \(\log_a(x)\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} x\amp =\amp a^{\log_a(x)}\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x]\amp =\amp \frac{d}{dx}[a^{\log_a(x)}]\\ 1\amp =\amp a^{\log_a(x)}\ln(a)\frac{d}{dx}[\log_a(x)]\\ 1\amp =\amp x\ln(a)\frac{d}{dx}[\log_a(x)]\\ \frac{d}{dx}[\log_a(x)]\amp =\amp \frac{1}{x\ln(a)}\text{.} \end{align*}
