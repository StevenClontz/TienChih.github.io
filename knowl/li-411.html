Item.
The average rate of change of \(R(t)\) over the interval \([1,3]\) is
\begin{align*} \frac{R(3)-R(1)}{3-1}\amp = \frac{60-40e^{-0.05(3)}-(60-40e^{-0.05(1)})}{2}\\ \amp = \frac{-40e^{-0.05(3)}+40e^{-0.05(1)}}{2}\\ \amp\approx 1.81042896. \end{align*}
So the average rate of change in the weekly revenue between 1 and 3 weeks is about \(\$1810.43\text{.}\)
