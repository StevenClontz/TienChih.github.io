Insulin affects the glucose, or blood sugar, level of some diabetics according to the function

\begin{equation*} G(x)=-0.2x^2 +450, \end{equation*}

where \(G(x)\) is the blood sugar level 1 hour after \(x\) units of insulin are injected. (This mathematical model is only approximate, and it is valid only for values of x less than about 40.)
