Item 9.3.5.4.
The probability that at least one person tested sick is \(1-\frac{C(15,3)}{C(20,3)}\text{.}\) Thus assuming this happens, the probability everyone tests sick is
\begin{equation*} \frac{C(5,3)/C(20,3)}{1-C(15,3)/C(20,3)}\approx \frac{0.0088}{0.6009}\approx 0.0146. \end{equation*}
