One last one, let \(h(x)=2^{\frac{x}{x+1}}\)

\begin{align*} h'(x)\amp =\amp \frac{d}{dx}[2^{\frac{x}{x+1}}]\\ \amp =_{CR}\amp 2^{\frac{x}{x+1}}\ln(2)\frac{d}{dx}[\frac{x}{x+1}]\\ \amp =_{QR}\amp 2^{\frac{x}{x+1}}\ln(2)\frac{(x+1)\frac{d}{dx}[x]-x\frac{d}{dx}[x+1]}{(x+1)^2}\\ \amp =\amp \frac{2^{\frac{x}{x+1}}\ln(2)}{(x+1)^2} \end{align*}
