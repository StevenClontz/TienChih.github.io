We can use the chain rule to find the derivative of log functions as well. Let's first compute \(\frac{d}{dx}[\ln(x)]\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} x\amp =\amp e^{\ln(x)}\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x]\amp =\amp \frac{d}{dx}[e^{\ln(x)}]\\ 1\amp =\amp e^{\ln(x)}\frac{d}{dx}[\ln(x)]\\ 1\amp =\amp x\frac{d}{dx}[\ln(x)]\\ \frac{d}{dx}[\ln(x)]\amp =\amp \frac{1}{x}\text{.} \end{align*}
