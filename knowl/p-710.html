Let's be a bit more general for just a moment, suppose that we had a function \(f(x)\) and we wanted to know what the instantaneous rate of change of a function was at some given \(x\text{.}\) Well, if we stepped forward by \(h\) units, we could measure the average rate of change of \(f(x)\) over \([x, x+h]\text{,}\) this would be

\begin{equation*} \frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{x+h-x}=\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\text{.} \end{equation*}
