The proof of this is a fairly sophisticated fact and it not trivial, but you can verify it by taking the slider \(h\text{,}\) dragging it towards 0 and seeing what happens: \url{https://www.desmos.com/calculator/4uv0zgguq9}. Using this fact, we can deduce:

\begin{align*} \frac{d}{dx}[e^x]\amp =\amp \limit{h}{0}\frac{e^{x+h}-e^x}{h}\\ \amp =\amp \limit{h}{0}\frac{e^xe^h-e^x}{h}\\ \amp =\amp e^x\limit{h}{0}\frac{e^h-1}{h}\\ \amp =\amp e^x\cdot1=e^x\text{.} \end{align*}
