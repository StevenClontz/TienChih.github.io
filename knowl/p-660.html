We can combine these rules to find the derivatives of some basic functions. So consider the derivative of \(f(x)=x^4-2x^2+7x-3\text{.}\) It would be:

\begin{align*} f'(x)\amp =\amp \frac{d}{dx}[x^4-2x^2+7x-3]\\ \amp =\amp \frac{d}{dx}[x^4-2x^2+7x^1-3x^0]\\ \amp =\amp 4x^3-2(2x^1)+7(1x^0)-3(0x^{-1})\\ \amp =\amp 12x^3-4x+7\text{.} \end{align*} in-context
