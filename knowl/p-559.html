So suppose you lend your friend some money at 2% weekly interest, and after 10 weeks they owe you $240, how much did you lend them? Well, we know that in this case \(r=0.02, w=10\) and the future value \(A=240\) so:

\begin{align*} A\amp = P+wrP\\ 240\amp = P+(10)(.02)P\\ 240\amp = 1.2P\\ P\amp = 200 \end{align*}
