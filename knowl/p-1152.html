Given \(n\) objects, the number of ways to select \(r\) of them without order, also called a combination is:

\begin{equation*} C(n,r)=\frac{P(n,r)}{r!}=\frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}. \end{equation*}

This is also commonly denoted \({n\choose r}\text{,}\) which we will avoid to prevent confusion with fractions.
