What we just observed is that if we let \(h\) converge towards 0, that it seems as if our quotient also converges to some value. This value is the derivative of \(f(x)\) at \(x\) and is denoted and defined as

\begin{equation*} f'(x)=\limit{h}{0}\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\text{.} \end{equation*}
