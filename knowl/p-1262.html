For the highs, we have, in order

\begin{equation*} 38, 38, 40, 43, 45, 50, 51, 59, 69, 77, 82, 85. \end{equation*}

So we have mean

\begin{equation*} \bar{x}=\frac{38+38+40+43+45+50+51+59+69+77+82+85}{12}\approx 56.1467. \end{equation*}

The median is the mean of the 6th and 7th entries \(\frac{50+51}{2}=50.5\text{.}\)
