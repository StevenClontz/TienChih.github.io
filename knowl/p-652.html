The derivative of \(f(x)=x^3\) is:

\begin{align*} f(x)\amp =\amp \limit{h}{0}\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\\ \amp =\amp \limit{h}{0} \frac{(x+h)^3-x^3}{h}\\ \amp =\amp \limit{h}{0} \frac{x^3+3x^2h+3xh^2+h^3-x^3}{h}\\ \amp =\amp \limit{h}{0} \frac{(3x^2+3xh+h^2)h}{h}\\ \amp =\amp \limit{h}{0} 3x^2+3xh+h^2=3x^2\text{.} \end{align*} in-context
