In this Chapter, we will introduce the notion of a linear function. Linear functions are used to model a variety of situations, such as the the revenue generated by selling a product, conversion between units of measurement such as Farenheit to Celcius, distance traveled by an entity moving at a constant velocity, demand of a product reelative to price and many more. It is also a good introduction to the theory of functions more generally.
