Similarly, the derivative of \(g(x)=\frac{x}{x^2+1}\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} g'(x)\amp =\amp \frac{(x^2+1)\frac{d}{dx}[x]-x\frac{d}{dx}[x^2+1]}{(x^2+1)^2}\\ \amp =\amp \frac{(x^2+1)(1)-x(2x)}{(x^2+1)^2}\\ \amp =\amp \frac{1-x^2}{(x^2+1)^2} \end{align*}
